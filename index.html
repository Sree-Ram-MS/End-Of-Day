End-of-Day Closing
Procedure

Step 01
Climate Control — AC Off
Switch OFF the AC using the remote and wait until the air ventilator closes completely before proceeding.
Step 02
Distribution Board (DB)
Go to the main DB area and gently push the door — it will open.
Step 03
Master Switches
Turn OFF all light switches and the AC master switch inside the DB.
Camera lights will turn ON automatically within 5 seconds — no action required.
Step 04
Washroom Check
Verify that washroom lights and the exhaust fan are switched OFF.
Step 05
Lock All Doors
Lock all doors using a Key or the Biometric system. For biometric locks, simply closing the door will lock it automatically.
Step 06
Stairway & Ground Floor Lights
Switch OFF all stairway lights and ground-floor lights.
Keep the three front bulbs ON.
Step 07
Kitchen Check
Check the kitchen area and ensure that the lights and fan are switched OFF.
Step 08
External Shutters
Close all shutters. Use the two iron rods provided to pull them down safely.
Step 09
Main Gate
Close the main gate securely.
Step 10
Secure Perimeter & Key Handover
Secure the area using the chain partition, then hand over all keys to the Security team at HQ.
