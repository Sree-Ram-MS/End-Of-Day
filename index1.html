Operations Manual

End-of-Day
Closing Procedure

Follow every step carefully to ensure the premises are properly secured at end of day.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Complete all steps in order before leaving the premises.

01

Turn Off the AC

Switch OFF the AC using the remote and wait until the air ventilator closes completely.

02

Access Main DB Area

Go to the main DB area and gently push the door — it will open.

03

Turn Off All Lights & AC Master Switch

Turn OFF all light switches and the AC master switch.
Note: Camera lights will automatically turn ON within 5 seconds — no action required.

04

Verify Washroom Lights & Exhaust Fan

Verify that the washroom lights and exhaust fan are switched OFF.

05

Lock All Doors

Lock all doors using either: Key, or Biometric system (for biometric locks, simply closing the door will lock it automatically).

06

Switch Off Stairway & Ground-Floor Lights

Switch OFF all stairway lights and all ground-floor lights, except the three front bulbs, which should remain ON. (Near kitchen)

07

Check the Kitchen

Check the kitchen and ensure that the lights and fan are switched OFF.

08

Close All Shutters

Close all shutters. Use the two iron rods provided to pull down the shutters safely.

09

Close the Main Gate

Close the main gate.

10

Secure with Chain Partition

Secure the area using the chain partition.

11

Hand Over Keys to Security

Hand over all keys to the Security team at HQ.
